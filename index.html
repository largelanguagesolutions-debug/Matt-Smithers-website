About

The short version.

I spent 22 years as a Navy Flight Engineer & Mechanic on P-3 Orion and C-130 Hercules aircraft, logging over 6,000 combat and operational flight hours. After the Navy, I moved into aerospace program management, supporting some of the most advanced defense platforms in the world at Northrop Grumman.


During that time, I've also been a Private Pilot, flying Bellanca Super Vikings, earned a Tailwheel Endorsement in a Vans RV-8 and earned my Airframes & Powerplant certificate in 2020.


Now I'm channeling all of that into building technology that brings general aviation into the modern era.
